Game Description

The little mexican Pepe wants to get his tasty taco back.
Fight the big evil chicken like you are hungry than never before.

Jump on the little chickens to defeat the chicken.

Collect the spicy bottles and throw them on the big chicken to beat it.
Beware, they are limited!

On your way to your taco you can collect coins.


Good luck and have fun!

Game Controls

Pepe Move Left
Pepe Move Right
Pepe Jump
Throw a bottle
