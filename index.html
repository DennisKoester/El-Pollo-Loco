El Pollo Loco

Game Description

The little Mexican Pepe want to be his taco back.
The evil big Chicken has stolen the Taco.

He want his Taco back.
Go to the evil big Chicken and retrieve his Taco.

Jump on the litle Chicken to defeat the Chicken.
Collect the Bottles and Throw they against the big Chicken to defeat him.
So you can get the tako in time for lunch.

Good luck and have Fun with this Funny Game.

Game Controls

Pepe Move Left
Pepe Move Right
Pepe Jump
Throw a bottle
